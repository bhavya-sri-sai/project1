Automated notes maker from audio recordings

In the present industry, communication is the key element to progress. passing on information to the right person and in the right manner is very important, not just on a corporate level, but also on a personal level.This can be accomplished by developing a voice recognition system speech-to-text which allows the computer to translate voice requests and dictation into text. The project is to able automated note makers from audio recording applications based on AI and ML to make the computer understand speech commands and convert them into text and then pdf/word format.
